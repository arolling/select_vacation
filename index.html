Choose Your Own (Vacation) Adventure

It is often difficult to decide which destination around the world to visit with your copious disposable income. Using this tool, you can find the best place to take a break from everyday life and observe quaint local culture.

Haiti

Haiti has a poor reputation as a tourist spot, but it offers white sand beaches and low costs just three hours' flight from New York. Within the context of the Carribbean, this is a high value for your dollar if navigated carefully. The fast-growing city of Beijing, China offers a gateway into the most populous country on Earth and all its cultural and historical treasures. Popular attractions include the Forbidden City, Ming tombs, Tiananmen Square, former Olympic sites, the Summer Palace, and many more. Cairo, Egypt

Egypt has been the home of a bustling civilization since ancient times. Visitors to Cairo can experience both the modern city and the ancient landmarks built by Pharoahs of old. Don't miss the Great Pyramids of Giza across the Nile River! Longer trips should include travel to the Valley of the Kings and the once-capital city of Amarna.
